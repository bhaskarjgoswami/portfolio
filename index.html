About me

Less about pixels,
more about me.

Hey, I'm Bhaskar. My design story began in the world of graphic design, where I played with colors, layouts, and branding magic. But over time, I got more curious about how things work, not just how they look. That curiosity led me into the world of UI/UX and I've never looked back.

Now, I design experiences that make sense, solve problems, and (hopefully) make someone's day a little easier. It's been a journey of pixels, people, and plenty of learning - and I'm just getting started.
